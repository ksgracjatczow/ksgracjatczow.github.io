The KS Gracja Tczów club is trying to acquire player Michał Andryczak, who is an offensive midfielder.

The player is 24 years old and currently plays in the second team of MKS Miedź Legnica, where he has scored 2 goals and made 7 assists in the current season. The player has already had experience in first league clubs, such as Jagiellonia Białystok, where he tried to break into the first team, but without success.

Throughout his career, the player has scored 29 goals and made 57 assists. Michał Andryczak has been playing since 2015 and has spent most of his career in second teams, such as Radomiak Radom and Pogoń II Szczecin. The player is left-footed and 183 cm tall, which allows him to handle the ball well.

The Gracja Tczów club sees a lot of potential in Andryczak and is trying to acquire him for their team. With his experience and skills, the player can contribute to the club's success and help in the fight for promotion to a higher league.
