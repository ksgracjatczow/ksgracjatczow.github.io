History of the club

KS Gracja Tczów is an amateur football club established in 2005. Initially the club wore green-black colors, now they wear red-black-white colors. The club has been playing in the local B-class league since its inception.

During the club's first few years, teams were created for the 1997, 1998 and 1999 age groups, as well as 2002 and younger. Through hard work and dedication of players and coaches, the KS Gracja Tczów team gradually began to achieve better results.

Currently, the main team of KS Gracja Tczów plays in the regional league, and the KS Gracja Tczów 2 team plays in the B-class. The club is constantly developing and striving for the best results, making it popular and respected by fans and the community.
