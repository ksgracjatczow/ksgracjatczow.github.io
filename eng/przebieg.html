The match between KS Gracją Tczow and LKS Zalesica was played at the Tczow stadium. From the very first minutes of the match it was clear that the hosts were definitely the better team. The fans of Gracja cheered their favorites, which further motivated the players to play even better.

The 6th minute of the match saw the first goal for KS Gracji, which came after a great action by the whole team. Another hit came as early as the 15th minute, this time after an individual action by one of the players. LKS Zalesice tried to respond, but their attacks were ineffective.

In the 25th minute of the match, the referee dictated a penalty kick for KS Gracji, which was successfully used, giving the hosts their third goal. Another hit was scored already in the 35th minute, and the fifth at the end of the first half. The score of the match at the break was 5:0 for KS Gracji.

The second half of the meeting did not bring as much excitement. LKS Zalesice still tried to make up for the loss, but their attacks were chaotic and ineffective. In the 75th minute of the match, a LKS Zalesice player saw a red card for a foul on a KS Gracji player.

In the end of the match, LKS Zalesice scored one more goal, but it was no longer able to change anything. The match KS Gracja Tczów vs LKS Zalesice was an exciting spectacle full of twists and turns. Gracja Tczów was definitely the better team, showing its advantage from the first minutes of the match. The fans of Gracja Tczów cheered their team, which only added to the energy of the players. In the first half of the match, Gracja Tczów scored three goals, thanks to the great play of the forwards and the effective play of the defense. However, LKS Zalesice fought until the end and scored a contact goal in the second half. Unfortunately, their efforts were thwarted by a red card for one of their players and the coach being sent to the stands for inappropriate behavior.
